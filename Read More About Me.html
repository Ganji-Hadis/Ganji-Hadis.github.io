About Me

Full Autobiography

Before University

I was Born in 1999 in Khomin, Markazi Province, Iran. I had the opportunity to showcase my exceptional talents during high school by successfully passing the entrance exam of the National Organization for Development of Exceptional Talents (commonly known as SAMPAD). As a result, I earned my math and physics diploma from this distinguished institution, achieving an impressive score of 17.20 out of 20. Subsequently, I successfully passed the entrance exam for the University of Isfahan., one of Iran's prestigious state-funded universities, where I pursued my studies in the field of computer engineering.

My Bachelor's Journey

During the process of selecting my university field, I lacked a clear and accurate understanding of the available options. Consequently, I made a decision based on an incomplete view of the fields and the job market prospects after graduation, leading me to choose computer engineering. However, upon entering the university, I didn't feel a strong interest in this field, and I found myself grappling with confusion about my true passions.It was not until I discovered the specialization of computer architecture that I finally found my genuine interest and enthusiasm. From that point on, everything changed. I became deeply engaged and motivated, which resulted in achieving exceptional grades in all the specialized courses related to this field.

Other Interests and Hobbies

From my earliest memories, I have been encouraged by the continuous support of my parents to engage in various non-academic activities, which allowed me to lead a well-rounded life alongside my studies. Among these activities, art played an important role, and I thoroughly enjoyed attending classes on oil painting and calligraphy. Additionally, I also explored my interests in sports, participating in classes for karate, swimming, and volleyball.
I initially started with swimming, and as I delved into the sport, I found a genuine interest in it. Later, I decided to pursue volleyball more seriously. These choices brought about several achievements at the provincial, national, and collegiate levels in both sports. These experiences have positively impacted my life, instilling qualities like discipline, perseverance, and a drive for excellence that have shaped my character and approach to challenges.

Through various chances, through all vicissitudes, we make our way ....
